# Conditions

## Scheduling
- Courses should't overlap
- There is 1 lecture for every 1 selected lab/tutorial
    - 1 or 0 labs/tutorials per lecture/section per course
- Allow people to remove entire hours of timetables
- Use RMP score as a 2nd objective (lexicographic)
- Consider commute times
- Consider other fixed events
- QoL:
    - Consider at most x courses in a FW/SU semester
    - Ensure classes are at most/least x minutes apart
    - Ensure RMP scores are at least x
    - Customize optimization for lexicographic or weighted sum (not really worth it)


### Courses
- Happen at earliest 8 AM, and at latest ~9 PM
- Cannot happen before prerequisites
- Redundant:
    - Cannot overlap
    - There is 1 lecture for every 1 selected lab/tutorial
        - 1 or 0 labs/tutorials per lecture/section per course
